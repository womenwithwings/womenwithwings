Welcome to Women with Wings!

During her flight training in 2020, Beth Ramirez created Women with Wings to support, recognize, and encourage women in aviation no matter where they are in their aviation journey.

Women with Wings is open to women of all ages and stages of their careers/training. We offer mentorship to ladies starting out in the industry and ambassadorship to promote our mission worldwide as well as scholarships.

We are also starting a new program, Girls With Wings, for girls ages 7-17. This first year will be completely online and we will send out an activity box every 3-4 months and work on the activities together once a month!
